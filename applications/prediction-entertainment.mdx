## The Entertainment Angle

<Note>
  Watching is passive. Predicting is participatory. The difference is engagement.
</Note>

Versus turns any event into interactive entertainment:

| Without Versus | With Versus |
|----------------|-------------|
| Watch the game | Watch YOUR prediction play out |
| Follow election news | Track YOUR position move |
| Wait for award show | Experience YOUR picks win or lose |
| Read market updates | Feel YOUR analysis validated |

## Social Viewing

Predictions enhance group experiences:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pre-Event">
    Everyone makes predictions before the event starts. Compare picks and talk trash.
  </Step>
  <Step title="During Event">
    Watch together as positions resolve. Celebrate and commiserate in real-time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post-Event">
    Review results, crown the winner, and schedule the next prediction party.
  </Step>
</Steps>
