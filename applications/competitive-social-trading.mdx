# Competitive Social Trading

Versus transforms prediction markets into a competitive arena where your insights become your edge.

---

## The Competitive Edge

<Note>
  In traditional markets, you trade against the crowd. On Versus, you compete against your friends.
</Note>

Competitive social trading combines:
- **Market mechanics** for accurate pricing
- **Social graphs** for meaningful competition
- **Gamification** for engagement and retention
- **Transparency** for learning and improvement

---

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build Your Knowledge">
    Develop expertise in topics you understand: sports, politics, entertainment, crypto, or culture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Form Your Network">
    Connect with friends and follow skilled predictors to create your competitive circle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Predictions">
    Put your insights into action across thousands of markets with real stakes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Challenge & Compete">
    Go head-to-head with friends and climb the leaderboards to prove your skill.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Learn & Improve">
    Review your history, analyze your edges, and refine your strategy over time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Competitive Formats

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Leaderboard Racing">
    **Climb the Rankings**
    
    Compete on global, category, and friend leaderboards:
    
    | Leaderboard | Competition Style |
    |-------------|-------------------|
    | Global | Marathon (all-time performance) |
    | Seasonal | Sprint (quarterly resets) |
    | Category | Specialist (topic expertise) |
    | Friends | Bragging rights (social circle) |
    
    <Tip>
      Focus on categories where you have genuine knowledge edge.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Head-to-Head">
    **Direct Competition**
    
    Challenge specific opponents:
    
    - Pick any market
    - Choose your side
    - Opponent takes the other
    - Winner determined by outcome
    
    Perfect for settling debates and proving points.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Tournaments">
    **Structured Events**
    
    Special competitive events:
    
    - Bracket-style elimination
    - Multi-round competitions
    - Prize pools and rewards
    - Limited entry periods
    
    <Info>
      Tournaments are announced in the app and via notifications.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Leagues">
    **Ongoing Competitions**
    
    Join or create leagues:
    
    - Private groups with custom rules
    - Seasonal standings
    - Regular payout schedules
    - Community-defined categories
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Competitive Strategies

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The Specialist" icon="bullseye">
    **Deep Knowledge, Narrow Focus**
    
    - Master 1-2 categories
    - Build reputation as an expert
    - Higher win rate in chosen areas
    - Trade less but trade better
    
    Best for: Subject matter experts
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The Generalist" icon="grid-2">
    **Broad Coverage, Volume Play**
    
    - Participate across all categories
    - Accumulate volume bonuses
    - Diversified risk
    - Find mispricings everywhere
    
    Best for: Curious minds with broad knowledge
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The Contrarian" icon="arrow-trend-up">
    **Against the Crowd**
    
    - Identify overpriced favorites
    - Bet against public sentiment
    - Higher variance, higher upside
    - Requires conviction
    
    Best for: Independent thinkers
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The Sniper" icon="crosshairs">
    **Selective, High-Conviction**
    
    - Few predictions, maximum size
    - Only bet when edge is clear
    - Patient waiting for opportunities
    - Big swings on certain calls
    
    Best for: Disciplined risk-takers
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
