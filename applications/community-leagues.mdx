---

## What is a League?

<Note>
  A League is a private leaderboard with custom rules, shared among a closed group of participants.
</Note>

Leagues include:
- Private standings
- Custom scoring rules
- Entry requirements
- Prize pools (optional)
- Seasonal resets

---

## League Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Friend Leagues">
    **Casual Competition**
    
    Perfect for:
    - Friend groups
    - Family competitions
    - Work teams
    - Fantasy sports style
    
    Features:
    - Easy creation (< 2 minutes)
    - Invite via link
    - Standard rules
    - Bragging rights focus
    
    <Tip>
      Friend leagues are great for office pools and holiday competitions.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Community Leagues">
    **Topic-Focused**
    
    Perfect for:
    - Online communities
    - Subreddit members
    - Twitter followers
    - Podcast audiences
    
    Features:
    - Larger member counts
    - Topic restrictions available
    - Public or private
    - Community moderation tools
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Competitive Leagues">
    **High Stakes**
    
    Perfect for:
    - Serious competitors
    - Cash prize pools
    - Tournament qualifiers
    - Skill-based competition
    
    Features:
    - Entry fees
    - Prize distribution
    - Anti-collusion measures
    - Verified standings
    
    <Warning>
      Competitive leagues may have entry requirements and regional restrictions.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Sponsored Leagues">
    **Brand Partnerships**
    
    Official leagues with:
    - Sponsor backing
    - Larger prize pools
    - Promotional integration
    - Special badges/rewards
    
    <Info>
      Apply to host sponsored leagues through the Versus Creator Program.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Creating a League

<Steps>
  <Step title="Name Your League">
    Choose a memorable name that reflects your community or theme.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Rules">
    Configure scoring, categories, and competition format:
    
    | Setting | Options |
    |---------|---------|
    | Scoring | PnL, Win Rate, ROI, Custom |
    | Categories | All, Specific topics only |
    | Duration | Ongoing, Seasonal, Event-based |
    | Entry | Open, Invite-only, Application |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Prizes">
    Define what winners receive:
    - Bragging rights only
    - Token prizes (from pool)
    - External rewards
    - Badges and recognition
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Members">
    Share your league via:
    - Direct invite link
    - Social media share
    - QR code
    - Username search
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Set a start date and let the competition begin!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Scoring Options

Customize how winners are determined:

| Method | Best For | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Total PnL** | Volume players | Raw profit wins |
| **Win Rate** | Accuracy focus | Highest % correct |
| **ROI** | Efficiency | Best return on capital |
| **Custom Points** | Game-like | Points per prediction outcome |
| **Head-to-Head** | Tournament | Bracket elimination |

---

## League Seasons

Most leagues run in seasons:

| Season Length | Best For |
|---------------|----------|
| Weekly | Quick competitions |
| Monthly | Regular engagement |
| Quarterly | Sustained competition |
| Event-based | Award shows, playoffs |
| Annual | Long-term bragging rights |

<Note>
  Seasonal resets keep competition fresh and give new members a fair chance.
</Note>
