## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit.

<Note>
  We also use similar technologies like local storage, pixels, and SDKs that function similarly to cookies.
</Note>

## Managing Cookies

### In Your Browser

Most browsers allow you to:
- View what cookies are stored
- Delete individual or all cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all cookies (may affect functionality)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Chrome" icon="chrome">
    Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Safari" icon="safari">
    Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Firefox" icon="firefox">
    Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Edge" icon="edge">
    Settings → Cookies and site permissions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### On Versus

Access your cookie preferences:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Select **Privacy**
3. Tap **Cookie Preferences**
4. Toggle categories on/off

<Warning>
  Disabling essential cookies will prevent the platform from functioning properly.
</Warning>
