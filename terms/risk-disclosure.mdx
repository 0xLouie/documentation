# Risk Disclosure Statement

**Last Updated: December 2025**

Please read this Risk Disclosure Statement carefully before using Versus. Prediction markets involve risk of loss.

---

## General Risk Warning

<Warning>
  Prediction markets involve significant risk of loss. You should only predict with funds you can afford to lose. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
</Warning>

By using Versus, you acknowledge that:

1. You may lose some or all of your tokens
2. Prediction outcomes are uncertain
3. No strategy guarantees profits
4. You are responsible for your own decisions

---

## Types of Risk

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Market Risk" icon="chart-line">
    **Uncertainty of Outcomes**
    
    Prediction markets are based on uncertain future events. No matter how likely an outcome appears, the opposite can occur.
    
    - Favorites lose
    - Upsets happen
    - Unexpected events change outcomes
    - Historical patterns don't guarantee future results
    
    *You may lose 100% of your prediction amount on any single market.*
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Liquidity Risk" icon="water">
    **Execution and Pricing**
    
    Market liquidity affects your ability to enter and exit positions:
    
    - Low liquidity markets may have wider spreads
    - Large positions may experience slippage
    - Some markets may have limited volume
    - Rapid market moves can affect execution
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Platform Risk" icon="server">
    **Technical Considerations**
    
    Technology systems involve inherent risks:
    
    - System outages or maintenance
    - Network connectivity issues
    - Software bugs or errors
    - Data transmission delays
    
    *While we strive for 99.9% uptime, no system is perfect.*
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Resolution Risk" icon="flag-checkered">
    **Outcome Determination**
    
    Market resolution involves potential issues:
    
    - Disputed outcomes
    - Unclear resolution criteria
    - Delays in official results
    - Retroactive changes to outcomes
    
    *Resolution processes are designed to be fair but may not satisfy all participants.*
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Regulatory Risk" icon="gavel">
    **Legal and Compliance**
    
    The regulatory landscape is evolving:
    
    - Laws may change in your jurisdiction
    - Platform access may be restricted
    - Features may be modified for compliance
    - Tax obligations vary by location
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Risk Mitigation

While risks cannot be eliminated, they can be managed:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Limits">
    Only predict with funds you can afford to lose completely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Diversify">
    Spread predictions across markets and categories.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Size Appropriately">
    Keep individual positions small relative to your bankroll.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay Informed">
    Research markets before entering positions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take Breaks">
    Step away after significant wins or losses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Responsible Use

Signs you should take a break:

- Spending more than intended
- Predictions affecting mood significantly
- Chasing losses consistently
- Neglecting responsibilities
- Borrowing to make predictions

Resources: If you or someone you know has a gambling problem, contact the National Council on Problem Gambling at 1-800-522-4700.
