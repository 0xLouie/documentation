## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information.

### We may share information with:

| Recipient | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **Service Providers** | Help operate our platform (hosting, analytics, support) |
| **Payment Processors** | Process transactions |
| **Identity Verification** | Verify user identities |
| **Legal Authorities** | When required by law or legal process |
| **Business Transfers** | In case of merger, acquisition, or sale |

### Public Information

The following may be visible to other users:
- Username and avatar
- Prediction activity (unless in Stealth Mode)
- Leaderboard standings
- Challenge results
- Badges and achievements

## 9. Regional Rights

### California Residents (CCPA)

You have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt out of sale (we don't sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising rights

### European Residents (GDPR)

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectification of inaccurate data
- Erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Data portability
- Object to processing
- Lodge a complaint with supervisory authority
