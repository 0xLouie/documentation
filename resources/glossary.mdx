# Glossary

Your complete guide to Versus terminology. Bookmark this page for quick reference.

---

## A

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AMM (Automated Market Maker)">
    The algorithm that sets odds and ensures liquidity on Versus. The AMM automatically adjusts prices based on prediction flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Active Position">
    A prediction you've entered that hasn't yet resolved. Tracked in your Portfolio Dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## B

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Badge">
    Visual recognition earned through achievements (win streaks, category expertise, milestones). Displayed on your profile.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bankroll">
    Your total available token balance for making predictions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## C

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Challenge">
    A direct head-to-head prediction battle between two users on a specific market. See [Vs Friends Challenges](/terminal/vs-friends-challenges).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Category">
    Topic classification for markets: Sports, Politics, Entertainment, Finance, Crypto, Culture.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Consistency Bonus">
    Extra ranking points earned for sustained positive performance over time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## E

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Entry Odds">
    The probability displayed when you entered your prediction. Your payout is based on these odds, regardless of later movement.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Entry Price">
    Another term for Entry Odds. The "price" at which you bought your position.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## F

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Feed">
    Real-time stream of activity from the platform, your network, or specific markets. See [Live Feed](/using/live-feed).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Friends">
    Mutual connections on Versus who can see your activity and challenge you directly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## L

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="League">
    Private competition group with custom rules and standings. See [Community Leagues](/applications/community-leagues).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Leaderboard">
    Rankings of users by various metrics (PnL, win rate, ROI, etc.). Global, category, and friend leaderboards available.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Limit Order">
    An order that only executes if the market reaches your specified odds target.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Liquidity">
    The depth of tokens available in a market. High liquidity = less slippage on large orders.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Locked">
    Market status when an event is in progress. No new predictions accepted; existing positions frozen.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## M

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Market">
    A prediction opportunity on a specific event or question. Each market has defined outcomes and resolution criteria.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Market Card">
    The UI component displaying market information in the Terminal. See [Market Cards](/terminal/market-cards).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Market Order">
    An order that executes immediately at current market odds.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## O

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Odds">
    The probability assigned to each outcome in a market, expressed as a percentage. Determines potential payout.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Open">
    Market status when predictions are being accepted and odds are moving.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Outcome">
    One of the possible results of a market (e.g., Yes/No, Team A/Team B).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## P

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payout">
    The amount you receive if your prediction is correct. Calculated as: Amount / (Odds / 100).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="PnL (Profit and Loss)">
    Your net result from predictions. Positive = profit, Negative = loss. See [How PnL is Calculated](/concepts/pnl-calculation).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Position">
    Your stake in a particular market outcome. Includes amount, entry odds, and selected side.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Portfolio">
    Your collection of active positions and historical predictions. See [Portfolio Dashboard](/using/portfolio-dashboard).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## R

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Realized PnL">
    Actual profit or loss from predictions that have resolved.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Resolution">
    The process of determining and confirming a market's outcome. Triggers payout to correct predictors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="ROI (Return on Investment)">
    Efficiency metric: (Total PnL / Total Amount Predicted) × 100%. Higher = more efficient.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## S

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Slippage">
    The difference between expected and actual execution price on large orders. Caused by moving through the AMM curve.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Social Betting Layer">
    The framework of social features (friends, challenges, leaderboards) that make Versus a competitive experience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Stealth Mode">
    Privacy setting that hides your predictions from friends until resolution.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Streak">
    Consecutive wins or losses. Win streaks earn bonuses; loss streaks may trigger risk warnings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## T

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Terminal">
    The main interface for browsing and entering predictions. Home of Market Cards and filtering tools.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Token">
    The unit of value used for predictions on Versus. Earned through activity, purchases, or referrals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Track Record">
    Your verified history of predictions, including win rate, categories, and performance over time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## U

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Unrealized PnL">
    Theoretical profit or loss based on current odds. Only realized when the market resolves.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## V

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Verified Profile">
    Account with completed identity verification, earning higher trust and access to premium features.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Volume">
    Total amount of tokens predicted on a market or by a user.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Versus Score (VS)">
    The composite ranking metric combining win rate, volume, consistency, and risk management.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## W

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Watchlist">
    Markets you're following without having a position. Useful for tracking before entry.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Win Rate">
    Percentage of predictions you got correct: (Wins / Total Resolved) × 100%.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
