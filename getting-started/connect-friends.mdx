## Finding Friends

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search">
    **Find Existing Users**
    
    1. Go to the **Friends** section in the Terminal
    2. Use the search bar
    3. Search by name or unique handle
    4. Send a friend request
    
    <Info>
      Searches are case-insensitive.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Invite Link">
    **Share Your Link**
    
    1. Go to **Profile â†’ Share**
    2. Copy your personal invite link
    3. Share via text, social, or email
    4. Friends who join are auto-connected
    
    ```
    versus.gg/invite/yourusername
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email Invite">
    **Direct Invitation**
    
    1. Go to **Friends â†’ Invite**
    2. Enter your friend's email
    3. We send them an invite
    4. They join and you're connected
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
