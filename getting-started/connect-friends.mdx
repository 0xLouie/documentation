## Finding Friends

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search by Username">
    The quickest way to connect:
    
    1. Tap the **Friends** icon in the navigation
    2. Use the search bar to find friends by username
    3. Send a friend request
    4. Once accepted, you'll see each other's activity
    
    <Info>
      Usernames are case-insensitive, so "CryptoKing" and "cryptoking" will find the same user.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Share Your Link">
    Share your unique invite link:
    
    1. Go to **Profile â†’ Share Profile**
    2. Copy your personal invite link
    3. Share via text, social media, or email
    4. Friends who join via your link are auto-connected
    
    ```
    versus.gg/u/yourusername
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Contacts Sync">
    Find friends already on Versus:
    
    1. Go to **Friends â†’ Find Contacts**
    2. Allow contact access (your data stays private)
    3. We'll show which contacts are on Versus
    4. Send friend requests with one tap
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Privacy Controls

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who can see my predictions?" icon="eye">
    By default, friends can see your positions after you enter them. You can enable "Stealth Mode" to hide positions until markets resolve.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who can challenge me?" icon="shield">
    Control who can send you challenges:
    - **Everyone**: Any Versus user
    - **Friends Only**: Only connected friends
    - **Nobody**: Disable incoming challenges
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I block someone?" icon="ban">
    Yes. Go to their profile, tap the menu (â‹¯), and select "Block User." They won't be able to see your activity or challenge you.
