## Setup Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Method">
    Visit the Versus app and select how you want to sign up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Username">
    Pick a unique username. This is how friends find you and how you appear on leaderboards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Profile">
    Add an avatar and bio. Your profile is your identity on Versus.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Your Alpha Badge">
    As a Season Zero participant, you get an exclusive Alpha Badge.
  </Step>
</Steps>
