## Step-by-Step Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Method">
    Visit [app.versus.gg](https://app.versus.gg) and select your preferred sign-up method. We recommend using Google or Apple for the fastest experience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Username">
    Pick a unique username that will represent you on leaderboards and in challenges. Choose wiselyâ€”this is how friends will find you!
    
    <Tip>
      Your username can be changed later in settings, but your prediction history stays with your account.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Avatar">
    Upload a profile picture or choose from our collection of avatars. A recognizable avatar helps friends spot you in the live feed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Account">
    Complete email verification to unlock all features including friend challenges and leaderboard eligibility.
  </Step>
</Steps>
