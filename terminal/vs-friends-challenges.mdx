# 1v1 Challenges

The heart of Versus. Challenge friends directly, stake your confidence, and settle who's right once and for all.

---

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a Real-World Outcome">
    Pick any open market—sports, politics, crypto, or culture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Challenge a Friend">
    Select an opponent from your network or accept an incoming challenge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stake Confidence">
    Both sides commit tokens. Higher stakes = higher rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic Resolution">
    When the event concludes, the result resolves automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Winner Takes All">
    Victor claims the pot and climbs the rankings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Challenge Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Direct Challenge">
    **Challenge a Specific Friend**
    
    - Pick a market
    - Choose your side (YES or NO)
    - Select your opponent
    - Set the stake
    - Send the challenge
    
    Your friend gets notified and has 24 hours to accept.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Accept Challenge">
    **Respond to Incoming Challenges**
    
    When someone challenges you:
    - Review the market and their side
    - See the stake amount
    - Accept to lock in, or decline
    
    <Info>
      Accepted challenges are locked—no backing out.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
