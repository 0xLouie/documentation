# Vs Friends Challenges

Challenges are the heart of social competition on Versus. Go head-to-head with friends on any market for ultimate bragging rights.

---

## What is a Challenge?

A challenge is a direct prediction battle between two users:

- **You pick the market**
- **You pick your side**
- **Your friend takes the opposite side**
- **Winner takes bragging rights (and stakes, if wagered)**

<Info>
  Challenges are more than just parallel predictionsâ€”they're a commitment to face off directly.
</Info>

---

## Creating a Challenge

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Market">
    Browse the Terminal and select a market you feel confident about.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Side">
    Decide which outcome you're predicting. You'll be locked into this choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an Opponent">
    Pick from:
    - Your friends list
    - Recent opponents
    - Anyone (open challenge)
    - Multiple friends (group challenge)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Stakes (Optional)">
    Choose what's on the line:
    - Bragging rights only
    - Token wager (matched by opponent)
    - Custom stakes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Challenge">
    Your opponent receives a notification and has 24 hours to accept.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Challenge Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Direct Challenge">
    **One-on-One Battle**
    
    - Challenge a specific friend
    - They must accept to activate
    - Private between you two (unless shared)
    - Stakes matched equally
    
    <Tip>
      Direct challenges have a 75% acceptance rateâ€”people want to compete!
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Open Challenge">
    **Anyone Can Accept**
    
    - Post a challenge without naming an opponent
    - First person to accept locks it in
    - Great for finding new competitors
    - Visible in the challenge feed
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Group Challenge">
    **Multiple Opponents**
    
    - Challenge up to 10 friends simultaneously
    - Each accepts independently
    - Creates multiple parallel challenges
    - Perfect for group debates
    
    <Info>
      Group challenges are great for settling arguments in group chats.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Revenge Match">
    **Rematch a Past Loss**
    
    - Automatically offered after challenge losses
    - Same opponent, new market
    - Double or nothing option
    - Settle the score
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Challenge Lifecycle

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pending" icon="clock">
    Challenge sent, awaiting response:
    - Opponent has 24 hours to accept
    - You can cancel before acceptance
    - Reminder sent at 4 hours remaining
    - Expires if not accepted
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Active" icon="circle-play">
    Challenge accepted, game on:
    - Both positions locked in
    - Visible in both users' active challenges
    - Can be shared to feed
    - Stakes held in escrow (if wagered)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Locked" icon="lock">
    Market locked, awaiting resolution:
    - No changes possible
    - Countdown to result
    - Trash talk still enabled ðŸ’¬
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Resolved" icon="trophy">
    Winner determined:
    - Result notification sent to both
    - Winner's stats updated
    - Stakes distributed
    - Rematch option offered
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Stakes & Wagering

<Note>
  Token stakes are optional. Many challenges are played for bragging rights alone.
</Note>

| Stake Type | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Bragging Rights** | No tokens, just glory |
| **Matched Stakes** | Both users wager same amount |
| **Custom Stakes** | Negotiated amounts (Pro) |
| **Double or Nothing** | Automatic on rematch |

### Stake Limits

| User Tier | Max Stake per Challenge |
|-----------|------------------------|
| Free | 500 tokens |
| Pro | 5,000 tokens |
