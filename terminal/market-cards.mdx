## Card States

Market cards change appearance based on status:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Open Market" icon="circle-play">
    **Active & Trading**
    
    - Full color, high contrast
    - Odds updating in real-time
    - All actions available
    - Pulse animation on recent activity
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Your Position" icon="bullseye">
    **You're In This Market**
    
    - Highlighted border in your prediction color
    - Your outcome emphasized
    - PnL preview shown
    - Quick-view of entry odds vs current
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Locked" icon="lock">
    **Event In Progress**
    
    - Muted colors
    - "Locked" badge overlay
    - Odds frozen at lock time
    - Countdown to resolution
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Resolved" icon="flag-checkered">
    **Outcome Determined**
    
    - Winning outcome highlighted in green
    - Losing outcomes greyed out
    - Final odds displayed
    - PnL shown for your position
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Card Layouts

Market cards adapt to different contexts:

| Context | Layout |
|---------|--------|
| **Terminal Feed** | Compact card, essential info only |
| **Market Detail** | Expanded view with full stats |
| **Portfolio** | Position-focused with PnL prominent |
| **Challenge** | Side-by-side with opponent's position |
| **Widget** | Minimal, single-outcome display |
