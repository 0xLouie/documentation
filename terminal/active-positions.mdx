## Position Information

Each active position displays:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Core Details">
    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | **Market** | The event you predicted on |
    | **Your Outcome** | Which side you chose |
    | **Amount** | Tokens committed |
    | **Entry Odds** | Price when you entered |
    | **Current Odds** | Live market probability |
    | **Unrealized PnL** | Potential profit/loss if resolved now |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Time Info">
    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | **Entry Time** | When you entered the position |
    | **Resolution Date** | When the market closes |
    | **Time Remaining** | Countdown to resolution |
    | **Status** | Open, Locked, or Resolving |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Market Context">
    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | **Total Volume** | Market-wide betting volume |
    | **Your % of Market** | How much of the market you represent |
    | **Friend Positions** | How friends are positioned |
    | **Odds History** | Chart of odds movement since entry |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Position States

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Open" icon="circle-play" defaultOpen>
    **Market Still Trading**
    
    - Odds continue to move
    - New participants can enter
    - Your unrealized PnL fluctuates
    - All position details visible
    
    <Info>
      This is where most of your positions will be. Watch the odds and wait for resolution.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Locked" icon="lock">
    **Event In Progress**
    
    - No new predictions accepted
    - Odds frozen at lock time
    - Waiting for event outcome
    - Position cannot be modified
    
    <Info>
      Sports markets lock at game start. Political markets may lock on election day.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Resolving" icon="hourglass-half">
    **Outcome Being Determined**
    
    - Event completed
    - Awaiting official result confirmation
    - Usually resolves within hours
    - Rare disputes may extend this period
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Position Actions

From any active position, you can:

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **View Market** | Go to full market detail |
| **Share** | Post your position to feed or social media |
| **Set Alert** | Get notified at specific odds levels |
| **Challenge** | Invite a friend to take the opposite side |
| **Hide** | Remove from main view (still tracked) |

---

## Watching Odds Movement

The mini-chart on each position shows:

- **Entry Point**: Marked line at your entry odds
- **Current Price**: Live odds position
- **High/Low**: Range since your entry
- **Trend**: Direction of recent movement

<Tip>
  Tap the chart to expand and see full historical odds with timestamps.
</Tip>
