## How to Enter a Market

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Markets">
    Scroll through available markets or filter by category
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Market">
    Click any market card to view full details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Side">
    Pick YES or NO based on your prediction
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Stake">
    Enter how much you want to commit
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review and submit your prediction
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Market Information

Each market detail page shows:

| Info | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Question** | Full description of the event |
| **Resolution Criteria** | How the outcome is determined |
| **Resolution Date** | When the market closes |
| **Current Odds** | Live YES/NO pricing |
| **Total Volume** | Amount staked across all users |
| **Your Position** | If you've already entered |

---

## Challenge from Markets

See a market you feel confident about? Challenge a friend:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Direct Challenge" icon="swords">
    Click "Challenge Friend" on any market, pick your side, select an opponent, and set your stake.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Open Challenge" icon="users">
    Post a challenge without naming an opponent. First friend to accept locks it in.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## Market Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Open** | Active, accepting predictions |
| **Locked** | Event in progress, no new entries |
| **Resolved** | Outcome determined, payouts processed |
