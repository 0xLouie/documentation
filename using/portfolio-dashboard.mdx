# Portfolio Dashboard

**Location:** `/terminal/portfolio`

The Portfolio Dashboard is your personal command center on Versus. Track your positions, monitor performance, and see how you're doing against friends—all in one place.

---

## Summary Cards

At the top of your dashboard, you'll see your key metrics at a glance:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Total Value" icon="wallet">
    Your current portfolio worth plus available balance
  </Card>
  <Card title="Total PnL" icon="chart-line">
    Profit and loss with percentage change
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active Challenges" icon="swords">
    Count of ongoing 1v1 bets against other users
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Active Positions

A detailed table listing all your open predictions:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Market Name** | The event you're predicting on |
| **Side** | Your position (YES or NO) |
| **Entry Price** | The odds when you entered |
| **Current Price** | Live market odds now |
| **Current Value** | What your position is worth |
| **PnL** | Profit or loss on this position |

<Tip>
  Click any row to see full market details and manage your position.
</Tip>

---

## Vs Friends Panel

The social heart of your dashboard—a dedicated panel showing your active 1v1 bets against friends:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What You'll See" icon="eye">
    - **Opponent**: Who you're competing against
    - **Market**: What you're predicting on
    - **Stake Amount**: How much is on the line
    - **Status**: Whether you're currently winning or losing
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Quick Actions" icon="bolt">
    - Challenge to a rematch
    - View opponent's profile
    - Share the matchup
    - See full challenge history
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## Using the Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Stats">
    Glance at the summary cards to see your overall performance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Positions">
    Scroll through your active positions to see what's in play.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Friend Battles">
    The Vs Friends panel shows all your head-to-head action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take Action">
    Enter new markets, challenge friends, or manage existing positions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
