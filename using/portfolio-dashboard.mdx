# Portfolio Dashboard

The Portfolio Dashboard is your home base on Versus. See all your active positions, historical performance, and key metrics at a glance.

## Key Sections

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Summary Card">
    The top card shows your most important metrics:
    
    | Metric | Description |
    |--------|-------------|
    | **Total Value** | Current value of all open positions |
    | **Total PnL** | Profit/loss across all resolved positions |
    | **Win Rate** | Percentage of predictions resolved in your favor |
    | **Active Positions** | Number of open predictions |
    
    <Tip>
      Tap the Summary Card to see a detailed breakdown by category.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Active Positions">
    See all your open predictions:
    
    - **Market Name**: The event you predicted on
    - **Your Position**: Which outcome you selected
    - **Entry Price**: The odds when you entered
    - **Current Price**: Live market odds
    - **Unrealized PnL**: Potential profit/loss if resolved now
    
    <Info>
      Positions are sorted by resolution date, with the nearest first.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Performance Chart">
    Visual representation of your prediction journey:
    
    - Track your cumulative PnL over time
    - See trends across different time periods
    - Identify winning and losing streaks
    - Compare against the market average
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Recent Activity">
    Your latest actions on Versus:
    
    - New positions entered
    - Positions resolved
    - Challenge results
    - Friend interactions
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Filtering & Sorting

Customize your view to focus on what matters:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="By Category" icon="folder">
    Filter positions by market category:
    - Sports
    - Politics
    - Entertainment
    - Crypto
    - Finance
    - Culture
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="By Status" icon="circle-check">
    View positions by their current state:
    - **Active**: Awaiting resolution
    - **Winning**: Currently profitable
    - **Losing**: Currently at a loss
    - **Resolved**: Completed predictions
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="By Time" icon="clock">
    Sort by different time horizons:
    - Resolving Today
    - This Week
    - This Month
    - All Time
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Performance Insights

<Note>
  Pro users unlock advanced analytics including category breakdowns, optimal position sizing suggestions, and predictive streak analysis.
</Note>

### Understanding Your Stats

| Stat | What It Means | Good Benchmark |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| Win Rate | % of correct predictions | > 52% |
| Avg Return | Mean PnL per prediction | > 5% |
| Sharpe Ratio | Risk-adjusted returns | > 1.0 |
| Max Drawdown | Largest losing streak | < 25% |
