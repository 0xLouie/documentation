## Ranking Metrics

Leaderboards can be sorted by different metrics:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Total PnL" icon="coins">
    **Default Ranking**
    
    Raw profit/loss across all predictions. Rewards high-volume traders who consistently win.
    
    *Best for: Measuring absolute performance*
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Win Rate" icon="bullseye">
    **Accuracy Ranking**
    
    Percentage of correct predictions. Minimum 20 predictions required.
    
    *Best for: Measuring prediction accuracy*
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="ROI" icon="chart-line">
    **Efficiency Ranking**
    
    Return on investment as a percentage. Shows who makes the most per token risked.
    
    *Best for: Measuring efficiency regardless of volume*
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Streak" icon="fire">
    **Hot Hand Ranking**
    
    Current consecutive wins. Resets on any loss.
    
    *Best for: Identifying who's on fire right now*
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Time Periods

View rankings across different timeframes:

| Period | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Today** | Positions resolved in the last 24 hours |
| **This Week** | Sunday to Saturday |
| **This Month** | Current calendar month |
| **This Season** | Quarterly (resets Jan, Apr, Jul, Oct) |
| **All Time** | Since your account was created |

<Note>
  Seasonal leaderboards offer the biggest rewards. Top performers each season earn exclusive badges and token prizes.
</Note>
