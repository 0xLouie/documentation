# Friends Network

**Location:** [versusmarkets.io/terminal/friends](https://versusmarkets.io/terminal/friends)

Your Friends Network is the foundation of social competition on Versus. Build your network and grow your competitive circle.

---

## Friend List

Your connected users appear in a list showing:

| Info | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Name & Avatar** | Their profile identity |
| **Status** | Online or Offline |
| **Quick Actions** | Challenge, view profile, remove |

<Tip>
  Online friends can accept challenges immediately.
</Tip>

---

## Finding Friends

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search">
    **Find Existing Users**
    
    Use the search bar to find other users by:
    - Display name
    - Unique handle
    
    Results show matching profiles with an "Add Friend" button.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Invite">
    **Bring New Users**
    
    Don't see your friends on Versus yet? Invite them:
    
    - **Direct Link**: Copy your personal invite link
    - **Email**: Send an invite directly from the app
    
    <Info>
      Friends who join via your link are automatically connected.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

## Managing Connections

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Send Friend Request" icon="user-plus">
    Click "Add Friend" on any profile. They'll receive a notification.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Accept Requests" icon="check">
    Incoming requests appear in notifications. Accept to connect.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Remove Friend" icon="user-minus">
    Remove connections anytime from their profile.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
