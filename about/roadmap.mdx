## Now → BETA (Invite-Only)

We're currently in closed beta, building the core social prediction experience.

- **Social layer** — Friends, invites, parties
- **Rankings + XP** — Track your prediction performance
- **Season Zero** — The inaugural competitive season
- **Private challenges** — 1v1 matches with friends
- **Referrals + waitlist** — Grow through word of mouth
- **Alpha badges** — Exclusive rewards for early adopters

<Note>
**Goal:** Prove engagement & social retention.
</Note>

---

## 3 Months → LAUNCH (Open)

Opening Versus to the public with full competitive features.

- **Public access** — Anyone can join
- **Leaderboards** — Global and friend rankings
- **Creator picks** — Curated predictions from top users
- **Trending events** — Popular markets surfaced automatically
- **Cosmetic rewards** — Earn visual customizations
- **Community events** — Collaborative prediction experiences

<Note>
**Goal:** Acquire mainstream users.
</Note>

---

## 6 Months → Monetization

Sustainable revenue without paywalls or pay-to-win mechanics.

- **Competitive passes** — Premium competitive features
- **Seasonal battle passes** — Tiered rewards each season
- **Cosmetics & profile themes** — Personalization options
- **Sponsored tournaments** — Brand-backed competitions
- **Influencer prediction rooms** — Creator-hosted markets
- **Premium leagues** — Exclusive competitive tiers

<Note>
**Goal:** Monetize without paywalls.
</Note>

---

## 6 Months → Mobile App

Native mobile experience for always-on predictions.

- **Native push notifications** — Real-time alerts
- **Live outcomes** — Watch results as they happen
- **Challenge notifications** — Never miss a friend's challenge
- **Shareable matches** — Easy social sharing
- **Activity feed** — Stay connected to your network

<Note>
**Outcome:** Prediction becomes always-on; major retention unlock.
</Note>

---

## 9 Months → Live Streaming Integrations

Bringing predictions directly into live content on Twitch, Kick, and YouTube Live.

- **Streamer-controlled predictions** — Hosts create markets in real-time
- **Real-time viewer participation** — Audiences predict together

**Examples:**
- "Who wins this round?"
- "Will I beat this level?"

<Note>
**Outcome:** Real-time engagement & creator-driven user acquisition.
</Note>

---

## 9 Months → Creator Ecosystem

Empowering creators to build prediction communities.

Creators can host:
- **Prediction rooms** — Custom market spaces
- **Challenges** — Exclusive 1v1 competitions
- **Tournaments** — Multi-round bracket events

<Note>
**Outcome:** Creator-led communities + scalable monetization.
</Note>

---

## 12 Months → Embeddable Predictions

Versus becomes infrastructure that lives everywhere.

Predictions inside:
- Discord
- Substack
- Telegram
- Newsletters
- Twitter threads

<Note>
**Outcome:** Versus becomes **infrastructure**, not just a platform.
</Note>
