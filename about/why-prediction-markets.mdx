# Why Social Prediction?

Prediction markets are powerful. But they have one fundamental problem: they're structurally anti-social.

---

## The Problem

Prediction markets today are structurally anti-social. They're designed like trading platforms: anonymous, transactional, and financial.

Users place bets in silence, with:

- No identity
- No social feedback
- No progression
- No ownership
- No reason to return beyond speculation

As a result, even the largest platforms struggle with long-term retention because users don't feel part of anything.

---

---

## What We're Building

Versus is constructing a new consumer product category: **Competitive Social Prediction**.

Instead of forecasting against a faceless market, users challenge real people: friends, creators, communities, and rivals. Turning opinions and outcomes into shared gameplay.

Similar to how Twitch made gaming social and Robinhood socialized trading, Versus socializes prediction. Opening it to millions who currently watch from the sidelines.
