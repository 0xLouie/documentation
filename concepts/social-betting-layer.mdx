# Social Betting Layer

The Social Betting Layer is what makes Versus different. It's the framework that turns solitary prediction into social competition.

---

## Core Philosophy

<Note>
  Predictions are more engaging when they involve people you know.
</Note>

Traditional prediction markets are efficient but cold. The Social Betting Layer adds:

- **Identity**: Real profiles, not anonymous wallets
- **Connection**: Friend networks and communities
- **Competition**: Leaderboards and challenges
- **Narrative**: Stories and bragging rights

## Social Signals

The Social Betting Layer surfaces information that helps you:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Friend Positions" icon="users">
    See where friends stand on markets:
    
    - Number of friends with positions
    - Which side they're on (if public)
    - Their entry timing
    - Their confidence level (stake size)
    
    **Use it to**: Validate your thesis or find contrarian opportunities
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Smart Money" icon="brain">
    Identify high-performing predictors:
    
    - Top 100 leaderboard activity
    - Category experts taking positions
    - High win-rate users entering
    
    **Use it to**: Follow sharp money or fade overconfidence
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Momentum" icon="fire">
    Track social trends:
    
    - Most discussed markets
    - Fastest growing positions

## Social Features by Market Type

Different markets emphasize different social elements:

| Market Type | Primary Social Feature |
|-------------|----------------------|
| **Sports** | Challenge friends who root for opponents |
| **Politics** | Debate-driven predictions with reactions |
| **Entertainment** | Shared experiences and watch parties |
| **Finance** | Expert following and smart money tracking |
| **Crypto** | Community alpha and whale watching |

## Privacy Controls

You control your social visibility:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Public Mode" icon="eye">
    - All predictions visible
    - Full profile accessible
    - Appear in suggestions
    - Challengeable by anyone
  </Card>
  <Card title="Friends Only" icon="user-group">
    - Predictions visible to friends
    - Profile restricted
    - Hidden from suggestions
    - Friends can challenge
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stealth Mode" icon="ghost">
    - Predictions hidden until resolved
    - Activity not in feed
    - Still earn leaderboard points
    - Can still challenge
  </Card>
  <Card title="Private" icon="lock">
    - Full privacy
    - No social visibility
    - Must opt-in to challenges
    - Solo experience
  </Card>
</Columns>
